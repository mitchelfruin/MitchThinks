The Site

I built the site using the R package blogdown for a few reasons:

  • To learn the (very) basics of web development.
  • To encourage myself to read (and actually think about what I’ve learned).
  • To use R for something that didn’t feel like work.

The site is still very much a work-in-progress but is split into two sections based loosely on whether I’ve had an idea or I’m just recapping someone else’s:

  • Posts.
    • Traditional blog posts at the intersection of topics that I’m interested in and trying to learn more about, including:
      • Programming.
      • Data Science (analytics, statistics, and machine learning).
      • Alternative approaches to economics (behavioural, complexity).
      • Evolutionary biology.
  • Summaries.
    • Short notes on non-fiction books, academic papers, and textbooks.
      • Focused on illustrating concepts using R.
      • Won’t be exhaustive.
