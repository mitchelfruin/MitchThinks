The Site

I built the site using the R package blogdown for a few reasons:

  • To learn the (very) basics of web development.
  • To post book summaries to encourage myself to read (and actually think about what I’ve read).
  • To use R for something that didn’t feel like work.

The site is still very much a work-in-progress (unfinished articles are marked with a *) but is broadly split into two sections:

  • Book summaries.
    • Selective summaries of non-fiction books.
    • Focused on trying to illustrate concepts using R, with a few of my thoughts thrown in, rather than providing an exhaustive summary or review.
  • Interests.
    • Random articles at the intersection of topics that I’m interested in and trying to learn more about, including:
      • Programming.
      • Data Science (analytics, statistics, and machine learning).
      • Alternative approaches to economics (e.g. behavioural, complexity).
      • Evolutionary biology.
