  1. Goal: GDP growth \(\rightarrow\) The Doughnut.

  1. Big Picture: Self contained market \(\rightarrow\) Embedded economy.

  1. Behavioural: Rational economic man \(\rightarrow\) Social, adaptable humans.
  • Summarise chapter.
  1. Complexity: Mechanical equilibrium \(\rightarrow\) Dynamic complexity.

  1. Inequality: Growth will even it up \(\rightarrow\) Distributive by design.

  1. Environment: Growth will clean it up \(\rightarrow\) Regenerative by design.
