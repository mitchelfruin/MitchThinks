Intro

Proposes 7 changes to bring economics into the 21st century.

  1. Goal: GDP growth \(\rightarrow\) The Doughnut.
  2. Big Picture: Self contained market \(\rightarrow\) Embedded economy.
  3. Behavioural: Rational economic man \(\rightarrow\) Social, adaptable humans.
  4. Complexity: Mechanical equilibrium \(\rightarrow\) Dynamic complexity.
  5. Inequality: Growth will even it up \(\rightarrow\) Distributive by design.
  6. Environment: Growth will clean it up \(\rightarrow\) Regenerative by design.
  7. Growth: Growth addicted \(\rightarrow\) Growth agnostic.

Focus on diagrams is brilliant. Well worth reading.
